UMN K100 Run67 - Crystal

The Ge crystal used in USD1 was "No.27 crystal (2014)" fabricated by Dongming Mai's group at USD. A paper desccribing the process of making similar crystals is here. The measured properties of this crystal can be found below.

  • Net Impurity vs. Radius \(N_A-N_D\) ~ 10^11cm^-3
  • N_D+N_A* = 5~7.5 x 10^11 cm^-3
  • N_D* = 1~3.5 x 10^11 cm^-3
  • N_A* = ~4 x 10^11 cm^-3

*"These numbers are estimated values based on impurity distribution in the crystal. We can only use Hall effect measurement to measure the net carrier concentration (NA-ND). To get the absolute amount for each impurity, we need to fit the impurity distribution in whole crystal by impurity segregation equation. The fitted results have some uncertainty." - Guojian Wang (USD)
